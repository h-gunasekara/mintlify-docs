## What can you build with the Catalog API?

- **AI Shopping Assistants** - Build conversational shopping experiences with natural language understanding
- **Product Comparison Tools** - Compare products across vendors with detailed attribute analysis  
- **Smart Recommendation Engines** - Power personalized product recommendations with rich product data
- **Commerce Intelligence Dashboards** - Analyze trends, pricing, and availability across markets
